1. Service Desk

The Transport Service Desk is located in Room 105 on Floor 1 of the General Service Building in the Residential Zone.
It provides information about Games-time traffic routes and shuttle bus schedules; accepts reservations of T3 Pool Vehicles; answers questions about shuttle bus services for team event training; helps the NOC delegations to contact dedicated vehicles; and provides city/public transport information.
