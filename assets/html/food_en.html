1. Location

Athletes Dining Hall in the Residential Zone

2. Service Desk

The Catering Service Desk is located in Room 105 on Floor 1 of the General Service Building in the Residential Zone.
Its provides catering inquiry service; accepts orders of box-meals for competition venues; assists the NOC delegations in purchasing meal vouchers; and coordinate and arrange special meal orders for NOCs (including meal orders for special activities and injured or sick athletes).

4. Special Services

A nutritional kiosk is available in the Dining Hall, providing services like nutrition consultation, meal orders for sick athletes and birthday party reservations by NOC delegations.

5. Dining

Athletes and team officials of AYG Nanjing 2013 shall have meals by presenting their AYACs with a mark of fork & knife.

6. Guest Dining

Meal vouchers shall be purchased or meals shall be reserved for guests by the Chef de Mission or the authorised representative in advance at the NOC Services Centre. Once sold, the meal vouchers cannot be changed or refunded.
Meal Voucher Prices: RMB 60/person for breakfast, RMB 95/person for lunch, RMB 95/person for dinner, and RMB 50/person for midnight snack.
